Your RpgBalancer is built with responsive web technologies, meaning it works great on mobile devices right out of the box!

## How to Access on Mobile
1. **Deploy** the app using the `DEPLOY_GUIDE.md`.
2. **Copy the URL** (e.g., `https://rpg-balancer.vercel.app`).
3. **Open** it in Chrome (Android) or Safari (iOS).

## "App-Like" Experience (PWA Style)
You can install the website as an app on your home screen to remove the browser bar and give it a native feel.

### iOS (Safari)
1. Open the URL in Safari.
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow up).
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4. Name it "RpgBalancer" and tap **Add**.

### Android (Chrome)
1. Open the URL in Chrome.
2. Tap the **Menu** (three dots).
3. Tap **"Add to Home screen"** or **"Install App"**.
4. Confirm by tapping **Add**.

## Mobile Features
- **Responsive Layout:** The grid system automatically stacks cards vertically on mobile.
- **Touch Controls:** Sliders and buttons are touch-friendly.
- **Persistence:** Your saved configurations work on mobile too (stored in your phone's browser storage).

## Limitations
- **Screen Space:** Complex grids (like the Combat Arena) might require scrolling.
- **Performance:** Heavy simulations might run slightly slower than on desktop.
